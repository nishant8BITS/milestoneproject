The Band

Lead Vocals

Nicola is no stranger to the stage and has over a decade of experience singing with some of Irelands most prestigious wedding bands. Her soulful, powerhouse voice really wows the crowd.

Backing Vocals

Nicola's sister Lynn brings sweet harmonies to the table, making those big wedding songs sound amazing. Did we mention the sister's also have synchronized dance moves?

Lead Guitar

Meet Dan the Man! His talent and dexterity allow him to conquer the most difficult guitar anthems. Hi favourite artists include: Guns’n’Roses, Bon Jovi, Rory Gallagher, Thin Lizzie, Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd.

Rythm Guitar

Bass

Drums
