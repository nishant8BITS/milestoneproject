WEDDING BAND AND CORPORATE SPECIALISTS FROM THE NORTH EAST

2morrow's Party are all about music & entertainment. We fill dance floors! We are best known for up-beat dance music, but we cater for every taste, including Rock, Current Top 40, Disco, 70's and 80’s classics, whatever it takes to get people onto the dance floor and partying.

We are proud to say that 2morrow's Party never use backing tracks or ‘fake karaoke-style’ recordings in their gigs- just real music, played by some of the best musicians in Ireland. Every wedding and event is different and we tailor the music to suit every occasion.

We are experts in wedding music and can help you choose your first dance song. We also provide the DJ afterwards, one less thing to worry about.
